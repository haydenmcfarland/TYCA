How To Play TYCA:


P2:

W - Forward
A - Rotate Left
S - Reverse
D - Rotate Right
E - Rotate Barrel
Space - Shoot

P1:

Similarly:
ArrowUp
ArrowLeft
ArrowDown
ArrowRight
/ - Rotate Barrel
Enter - Shoot

Can Use GamePad For Both:

If Buffalo SNES GAMEPAD:

Shoot - A
Rotate Barrel - B
Forward - X
Reverse - Y
Rotate Left - Left Shoulder
Rotate Right - Right Shoulder

Otherwise, may need to rewrite controls in Unity.
